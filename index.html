About Us

Family-Owned Since 1986

Navan Hire, Hardware & Plumb Centre is located on the Kells Road, Navan. Established in 1986, we've spent nearly 40 years building our reputation as the North East's most trusted one-stop shop for hire, hardware, DIY and plumbing.

In 2012 we joined the Topline Group, allowing us to offer trade and non-trade customers the most competitive prices on the market.
